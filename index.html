Biography

I’m a student of chemistry and mathematics at the University of Colorado: Boulder. Currently I work as an Undergraduate researcher, with a focus on computational biology. I work jointly in the Taatjes and Dowell labs at CU. My work in the Taatjes lab focuses on the analysis of genomic data related to eRNA activity and the CDK9 and CDK19 mediator kinases, while my work in the Dowell lab works on development and improvement of tools for analysis of genetic sequencing data.
